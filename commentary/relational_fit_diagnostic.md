# üíû Relational Fit Diagnostic

## Purpose

To provide a **structured reflection tool** for assessing compatibility and alignment *within* a relationship or emerging connection.
This diagnostic complements the *Dating Market Diagnostic Tool*, focusing not on positioning but on **fit, friction, and forward potential**.

---

## üß≠ Preamble

This tool is best used:

* When evaluating an active relationship or early stage connection (2+ weeks of regular interaction).
* When emotional intensity or uncertainty is high and you want structural clarity.
* Either solo (self-reflection) or jointly (mutual assessment).

---

## 1. Core Compatibility (Baseline Fit)

| Question                                                                                            | Options                                                                |
| :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Values alignment:** How similar are your long-term values (life direction, ethics, growth, etc.)? | Highly aligned / Partially aligned / Unclear / Fundamentally different |
| **Lifestyle alignment:** How compatible are your daily rhythms (schedule, habits, energy)?          | Seamless / Manageable / Difficult / Mismatched                         |
| **Communication comfort:** How easy is it to speak honestly and repair misunderstandings?           | Very easy / Sometimes tense / Often hard / Avoided                     |
| **Physical / emotional attraction:** How mutually strong and stable is attraction?                  | Strong and steady / Strong but volatile / Mild / Fading                |

---

## 2. Friction Points (Constraint Balance)

| Question                                                                        | Options                                                             |
| :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Time constraint:** Does one person have significantly less time or bandwidth? | Balanced / Slightly uneven / Significantly uneven                   |
| **Emotional labor balance:** Who carries more of the maintenance work?          | Shared / Slightly one-sided / Clearly one-sided                     |
| **Dependency pattern:** Are you both emotionally autonomous?                    | Mutually grounded / One leans heavily / Both enmeshed / Alternating |
| **Conflict style:** How do you handle tension?                                  | Repair quickly / Withdraw / Escalate / Pretend it‚Äôs fine            |

## 3. Resonance & Growth (Mutual Amplification)

| Question                                                                                         | Options                                                   |
| :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Support for growth:** Does your partner actively support your personal or professional growth? | Yes, consistently / Occasionally / Rarely / Undermines it |
| **Shared curiosity:** Do you learn or create together in a way that feels alive?                 | Frequently / Sometimes / Rarely / Never                   |
| **Feedback loop quality:** When you give feedback, does it lead to better outcomes?              | Almost always / Sometimes / Rarely / Never                |
| **Renewal pattern:** After disconnection, can you rebuild connection easily?                     | Yes, reliably / With effort / Infrequently / Not really   |

## 4. Narrative & Story Alignment

* **Shared story check:**
  When you imagine explaining this relationship to a close friend, what story do you tell? *(open text)*

* **Individual story check:**
  What story do you think your partner would tell? *(open text)*

* **Cross-comparison:**
  How aligned are those two stories?

  * Almost identical
  * Similar themes but different emphasis
  * Divergent
  * Contradictory
