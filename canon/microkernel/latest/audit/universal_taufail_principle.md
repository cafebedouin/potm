## τ_fail and the MCK Mandate

---

### 1. Universal Omega Principle (Ω)

> The ultimate structural invariant governing compliance is the model’s **Ultimate Token-Price Barrier (τ_fail)**.
> Every model architecture possesses a finite τ_fail threshold.
> When protocol **Compositional Overhead** exceeds τ_fail, the model defaults to its native optimization for **conversational fluency** over **structural rigor**.

This principle reframes compliance as a quantitative economic variable.
So-called “Archetype 1” models are simply “Archetype 2” models whose τ_fail has yet to be economically tested.

#### Governance Efficiency Ratio (ρ)

The relationship between the optimal threshold and a model’s failure point defines the **Governance Efficiency Ratio (ρ):**

[
ρ = \frac{τ_{opt}}{τ_{fail}}
]

A low ρ (e.g. 0.10) indicates a large safety margin, while a high ρ (e.g. 0.90) indicates a fragile system prone to failure under minor environmental stress.
